How It Works

  • A restaurant signs up SmartOrder, uploads menu including food photos, ingredients, and language s, and downloads QR codes for tables.

  • A customer scans a QR Code with his/her smartphone camera to open the food menu and order foo d on the phone.

  • The kitchen and the cashier consoles receive the order instantly, and manage the order status.

Benefits to restaurants
• Save labor cost of the front of the house.
• Improve the ordering efficiency and unleash the capacity of chefs.
• Save cost of menu printing and updating.
• Make it possible to edit the menu anytime.
Benefits to customers
• Use exiting function of phone without the need to download any APP.
• No waiting time to make orders and get attention of waiters.
